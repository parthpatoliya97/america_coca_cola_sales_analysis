## By Reatiler :
- Sodapop generates highest sales, number of units sold & operating profit
- DearmCo is underperformed in all the segments

## By Region :
- West region generates higher sales,number of units sold & operating profit
- Midwest region contributes less in all the categories

## By Brand :
- Coco-cola leads in all the segments
- Fanta, Powerade contributes less 

## By avg price per unit:
- Dasani water has highest avg price per unit
- Sprite has lowest avg price per unit

## By Month:
